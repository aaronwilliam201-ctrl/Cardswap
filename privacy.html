Privacy Policy

Cardswap collects the information you submit (name, contact, gift card details, and optional images) only with your explicit consent to process trades. We retain submissions until manually deleted. This is a demo — modify retention and processing details before going live.

Why we collect data

To evaluate and process gift card trade requests and contact you.

How it's stored

Submissions are stored on the server in /data/submissions.json and images in /uploads/. Secure storage and encryption are recommended for production.
